Yoga Based Trauma Informed Care Psychotherapy approach:
For your journey, mindfulness based trauma treatment  techniques are part of your sessions. Many of those techniques supported by the American Psychological Association (APA) and Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services America  (SAMHSA)  and the United States Association for Body Psychotherapy (USABP) . 

The mindfulness base treatment is a Trauma Informed Care (TIC) that follows the national health care guidelines. TIC is only designated by a therapist who counts with certifications and studies from different Trauma Centers with high standards.
 Maya Moon counts with different organizations supporting our TIC nomination, including The Trauma Institute and the National Child and Trauma Center.
