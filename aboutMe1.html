About Me

PSYCHOLOGICAL TREATMENT BODY-MIND YOGA BASED PSYCHOTHERAPY.

Ilse Jimenez, LMHC, Somatic Practitioner - trauma specialist - Yoga Trauma Informed Care.

Ilse is a Latino provider born in Costa Rica, with a great appreciation for life, energy and nature. Her philosophy has been considered different when she observes opportunities for treatment involving the body-mind connection. Ilse utilizes the somatic psychotherapy approach to decrease the body mind reactions caused by traumatic experiences. Mindfulness techniques can assist you to rebuild the present moment without being anymore in threat response. Building this capacity is extremely important in the process of deeper healing. Ilse counts with great experience when noticing your nervous systems, her high intuition will help you to recognize trauma stuck in your body. Ilse's somatic training and degree in psychology brings a safe understanding of your freeze, fight, and flight respond. Born in a Tropical country, Ilse allowed herself to have contact with the Mother Earth and the nature elements, her spirituality departure from the belief that nature can help to heal trauma stuck (Eco-Therapy). Healing with the spirits of the Mother Earth is supported with walking meditation, contemplation, construction of mantra, including positive self-affirmation, sound healing, and more.
